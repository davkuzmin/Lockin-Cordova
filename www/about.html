What is Lockin?

At Lockin our goal is to create the secure internet platform of the future. In a time of uncertainty with the future of the internet, we want to be the constant that people can depend on to not only safely store what matters to you but to have a place where anyone can browse, share, create, and explore without fear of compromised identy or data. The internet is ours and should always remain that way, and Lockin is here to make sure that happens. Welcome to the internet of tomorrow, available today.
